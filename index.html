Ribollita Recipe

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Vegetable stock cubes
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Kale
  • Diced tomatoes
  • White cooking wine
  • White beans
  • Black pepper
  • Loaf of bread
  • Daiya Vegan Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Chop 2 cups of carrots
  2. Chop 2 cups of celery
  3. Chop 2 cups of kale
  4. Dice 2 cups of onions
  5. Mince 1 tbsp of garlic
  6. Saute the onions on low heat until they are translucent
  7. Then add the garlic to the onions
  8. Add the carrots, celery, and kale to the pan
  9. Then add 28 oz of diced tomatoes, followed by a splash of white cooking wine
  10. Add 2 cans of white beans, reduced for 3-4 minutes
  11. While reducing, boil 3 vegetable stock cubes in 6 cups of water in a large pot
  12. Add the pan containing the vegetables to the pot with the vegetable stock cubes
  13. Add 2 tbsp of black pepper to the pot
  14. Cook closed on a low simmer for 4-6 hours
  15. Break the loaf of bread into small pieces and add into the pot
  16. Cook on a low simmer for an additonal 3 hours, stirring every 30 minutes
  17. Add 1/2 cup of daiya fake parmesan cheese
